The Glitched Labyrinth

The Web was not always broken.

Once, its foundations were clean and orderly. Structure gave it form. Style gave it beauty. Logic gave it life.

But something went wrong.

Deep within the unseen layers of the network, a corruption began to spread. Pages fractured. Styles collapsed. Scripts spiraled into chaos. At the center of it all, the Web Core — the engine that binds everything together — flickers on the edge of failure.

From within the labyrinth of interconnected nodes, a rogue entity has emerged:

**Wumpus.exe**

It feeds on broken markup. It thrives in unstable logic. It hides somewhere in the maze.

You are a Debugger — a guardian of the system — sent into the Glitched Labyrinth to restore order.

Inside you will find:

  • Silent nodes and unstable chambers
  • Hidden traps and volatile surges
  • Fragments of lost code
  • A single Patch Script capable of destroying corruption

But be warned:

You cannot see beyond the room you occupy. You must navigate by instinct. You must interpret the signs around you.

If you enter a corrupted chamber unprepared, the system will collapse.

Your Mission

  • Explore the 16 interconnected nodes.
  • Locate and eliminate Wumpus.exe.
  • Find the Web Core.
  • Restore balance before the corruption consumes everything.

If you fail, the labyrinth resets.

The Web depends on you.
